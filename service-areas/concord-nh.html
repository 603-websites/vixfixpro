About Concord

Concord Homes Deserve Expert Care

Concord is New Hampshire's state capital and Justin Vickery's home base. The city offers a rich mix of housing: beautifully preserved Victorian homes in the South End near Pleasant Street, working craftsman bungalows around Eagle Square, and newer construction spreading north toward Canterbury Road. Justin has been working in Concord's neighborhoods for years and knows the quirks of this housing stock — from the original hardwood floors that just need refinishing to the century-old plaster walls that crack and settle every winter. When you call VixFix from Concord, you get a contractor who's already familiar with your neighborhood and can often schedule a same-day visit for estimates.
