About Londonderry

Londonderry: Growing Town, Growing Needs

Londonderry is one of southern New Hampshire's fastest-growing towns, situated between Manchester and Derry along I-93. The town is largely defined by subdivisions of colonials and garrison-style homes built from the 1980s through the 2000s, though older farms and village-center properties add variety to the housing mix. With continued residential growth, renovation and home improvement needs are high throughout Londonderry — flooring updates in aging builds, water damage repairs from ice dams and frozen pipes, and fresh interior paint for homes being prepared for sale or refinanced. Justin serves Londonderry regularly and knows the housing stock here well.
