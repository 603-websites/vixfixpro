About Nashua

Nashua Homes Deserve Better Service

Nashua is New Hampshire's second-largest city and one of the fastest-growing communities in New England. The city blends older downtown neighborhoods — colonial-era homes near Mine Falls Park and working-class housing along Main Street — with newer subdivisions pushing south toward the Massachusetts border. Whether you own a 1920s two-family in the Millyard district or a 1990s colonial in a south Nashua neighborhood, VixFix brings the same commitment to quality craftsmanship. Justin serves homeowners throughout Nashua's varied neighborhoods, responding fast to everything from water damage emergencies to planned flooring upgrades and full interior repaints. No runaround, no surprises — just honest work at a fair price.
