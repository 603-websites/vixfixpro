About Merrimack

Merrimack Homes Ready for a Refresh

Merrimack sits between Manchester and Nashua along the Merrimack River — one of southern New Hampshire's most convenient communities for Route 3 commuters. The town grew substantially in the 1980s and 1990s with colonial and split-level homes filling in subdivisions across the highway corridors. Many of these properties are now hitting the 30–40 year mark, which is the perfect time for a significant refresh: exterior paint that's showing its age, flooring that's been lived on hard, and basement moisture issues that come with river-adjacent living. Justin knows this housing stock well and serves Merrimack homeowners with the same responsive, quality-first approach he brings to every town he works in.
