About Derry

Derry Homes: Old Character, New Needs

Derry is a quintessential New Hampshire town, a mix of rural farmland, the historic Derry village center, and newer subdivision growth along Route 28 and Route 102. Homes here range from 200-year-old farmhouses near East Derry Road to 1990s colonials in planned developments. The old properties carry the charm of NH's past but need consistent upkeep to ward off water infiltration, drafts, and aging finishes. Newer builds, meanwhile, are reaching the age where flooring needs refreshing and drywall shows its first cracks. Justin serves Derry regularly and brings the same level of care to a historic farmhouse restoration as he does to a straightforward colonial repaint.
