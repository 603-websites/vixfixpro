The Right Way to Paint

Great Paint Jobs Start With Great Prep

A fresh coat of paint is one of the fastest ways to transform a room — but only if it's done right. The difference between a paint job that looks great for a week and one that looks great for a decade comes down to preparation: filling nail holes, sanding rough patches, caulking gaps around trim, and priming before color. Most homeowners don't see the prep — they just see the result. Justin does the prep right so the result holds up.

VixFix serves homeowners across New Hampshire, from Manchester and Nashua to Concord and the surrounding towns. Whether you're refreshing a single bedroom, painting an entire floor, or tackling trim and ceilings that haven't been touched in years, Justin gets it done cleanly and on schedule.
