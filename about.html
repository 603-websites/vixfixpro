Justin Vickery built VixFix on a simple belief: every job — no matter the size — deserves the same care and craftsmanship. That standard is what sets VixFix apart.

Meet Justin

The Craftsman Behind VixFix

Justin Vickery is the owner and operator of VixFix Professional Services LLC — a skilled, multi-trade craftsman with hands-on experience across painting, carpentry, drywall, framing, and major renovations.

He handles each project personally — from the first call to the final walkthrough — and holds every job to the same high standard regardless of size. When a project calls for additional hands, Justin brings a trusted network of skilled subcontractors to get it done right.

VixFix serves homeowners across New Hampshire with a wide range of services: painting, carpentry, drywall, flooring, water damage restoration, TV mounting, window treatments, furniture restoration, and more.
