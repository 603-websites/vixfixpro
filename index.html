Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas does VixFix serve?

VixFix is based in Concord, NH and serves homeowners throughout New Hampshire and northern Massachusetts, from the Lakes Region down through Manchester, Nashua, and Salem. If you're within about an hour of Concord, you're covered. See our full service area list.

Are you licensed and insured?

VixFix is fully insured and a lead safe certified firm. Owner Justin Vickery is a direct EPA lead safe certified technician. We work with subcontractors who carry licenses where required in NH and maintain liability insurance for their trade. Certificates of insurance available on request.

Do you offer free estimates?

Absolutely. Every estimate is free, no-obligation, and in writing so you can compare bids confidently. Call 603-202-5309 or fill out the form above.

How fast can you start?

Most estimates come back within 24 hours, and non-emergency projects typically get scheduled within 1–2 weeks. For active water damage or urgent safety issues, we aim for same-day or next-day service.
