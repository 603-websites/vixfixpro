About VixFix

Heart & Soul in Every Project

VixFix Professional Services LLC was founded by Justin Vickery — a multi-trade craftsman with years of hands-on experience across painting, carpentry, drywall, framing, and major renovations.

Justin's philosophy is simple: "Putting heart and soul into every project is essential to achieving a polished, professional result." That mindset drives everything from the smallest repair to a full-scale renovation.

We've expanded our capabilities to serve homeowners with a wider range of needs — including TV mounting, window treatments, and even automotive maintenance — backed by a growing network of skilled subcontractors ready for any project size.
