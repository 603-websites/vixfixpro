Fire Alarm & Smoke Detector Installation in NH

Hardwired replacements, battery-operated swaps, and full interconnected systems — installed correctly and up to NH code.

Why It Matters

NH Code Requires It. Your Family Deserves It.

New Hampshire law requires working smoke detectors on every level of your home — including the basement and inside or immediately outside every sleeping room. Carbon monoxide detectors are required wherever there's a fuel-burning appliance, fireplace, or attached garage. These requirements exist for a reason, and a detector that's past its service life, improperly placed, or the wrong type for the location can fail the one moment it matters most.

Justin at VixFix installs smoke and CO detectors correctly — the right type, in the right location, wired properly. No guesswork, no missed bedrooms, no outdated units left in place.
